About Us

Hello and welcome to Taste of America, an up and coming restaurant that does food right. We make everything fresh to order and exactly how you want it. In fact special requests are our favorite things! We wanted to ensure quality food without a long wait time so we've perfected a small menu so we can do it right, the first time. Our own is a super picky eater and she wanted to ensure that everyone felt right at home even if they are on vaction!

Let us help you get a Taste of America by giving us a call or requesting one from our contact page. We're excited to share our favorite foods with you and your family or large group (just give us a heads up)!
